Interactive Fiction Engine

Apr 21 2018

Turning a pick-your-own-adventure book into a video game is more difficult than I thought.

Right now, my idea is to use a conveyor belt-like structure to carry events [With flavour text FLV, choice CHC and consequence CNQ] in order, based on a time variable t.

t = 0 : FLV [0]
t = 1 : FLV[1]
t = 2 : CHC[0]
t = 3 : CNQ[0]
t = 4 : FLV[2]

And storing each of these FLV/CHC/CNQ elements in their own arrays, with the CHC/CNQ arrays paired to each other, where CNQ[i] is the consequence resulting from choice CHC[i].
It's probably an overcomplicated way of trying to make everything modular but I can't think of anything else...
